Anti-Patterns in OOP

oop anti-pattern

Here they come:

  • NULL References

  • Utility Classes

  • Mutable Objects

  • Getters and Setters

  • Object-Relational Mapping (ORM)

  • Singletons

  • Controllers, Managers, Validators

  • Public Static Methods

Avoid them at all cost.
