About Me

I'm Yegor Bugayenko, a software architect, passionate about software quality. I write mostly in Java, but also in PHP, Ruby, Python and JavaScript. I'm a co-founder and CTO of teamed.io.

Once in a month I'm sending a summary email of my writing activity. If you're interested to know what's going on here, I can add you to my mailing list. Just give me your email address and briefly explain who are you and why you're interested (I'm trying to avoid anonymous subscribers in my list).
